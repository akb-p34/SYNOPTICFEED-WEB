## Slide 9 — Business Model & Pricing (Pilot → Pro)

**Pilot (Lite)**
- **Free, 60–90 days.** LOI required. Scope: dashboard + 1 sandbox API. Slack support.

**Pro (Annual, team‑based)**
- **Desk – $150k/yr:** ≤5 users, 2 API keys, 1 region bundle.
- **Pod – $225k/yr:** ≤15 users, ≤3 teams, 4 API keys, 2 region bundles.
- **Firm – $350k/yr:** ≤50 users, unlimited teams (one entity), 8 API keys, all U.S. regions.

**Add‑ons (common)**
- Co‑lo/cross‑connect **$4k/mo** (+ **$25k** setup) · Priority SLA **+$25k/yr** · Soft exclusivity **+$50–100k/yr**.

**Terms**
- Annual upfront (Net‑30). Pilot $0 (no revenue assumed).

**Chart (Pricing Ladder)**  Pilot $0 → Desk **$150k** → Pod **$225k** → Firm **$350k** (tick at **$400k** for add‑ons).
